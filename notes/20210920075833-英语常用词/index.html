用作委婉#

背景：the man in the photo。 对比以下两句

  1. He is in good health
  2. He seems to be in good health(这句是错的 He seems in good health)

表示犹豫#

  1. That’s not right
  2. That doesn’t seem right
  3. That doesn’t seem to be right
